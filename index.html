The Smashing Pumpkins

ABOUT

The Smashing Pumpkins is an American Alternative Rock band. The group started making music in 1988 in Chicago, Illinois. The band currently has a total of four members. These members are James Iha, Kiki Wong, Jimmy Chamberlin, and Billy Corgan. Corgan is the band's guitarist and lead vocalist, Wong plays the guitar when on tour, Chamberlin plays the drums, and Iha plays the guitar as well.

The band has won many awards throughout the years. They won about eight MTV awards in 1996. They also won a Grammy for Best Hard Rock Performance in 1998. Lastly, the band won an American Music Award for Favorite Rock Artist. The Smashing Pumpkins was also influneced by many other bands such as Black Sabbath, Pantera, New Order, and The Cure.
